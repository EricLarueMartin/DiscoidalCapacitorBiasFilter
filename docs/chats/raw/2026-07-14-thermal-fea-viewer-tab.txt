The Designer viewer gained a Thermal FEA tab showing the solved epoxy
maximum-principal tensile-stress distribution. The FEniCSx backend returns
cell-center stress points from its exact half-stack solve; the browser mirrors
them for a full-stack display, caps the color scale at P99, and marks the raw
mesh-sensitive peak separately.

The old Geometry half-section tab was renamed FEA model regions. Its caption
now says it shows representative r-z material regions used by the
cylindrically symmetric FEA models rather than claiming to be a literal
half-section. The default thermal response contains 2,362 epoxy points and is
about 75 kB.
