# Agent Guide: docs/chats

Store collaboration history here.

- Put unedited exports or raw copied conversations in `raw/`.
- Put cleaned summaries, decision records, and hierarchical knowledge extracts in `markdown/`.
- At the end of any substantive design/coding session, add or append a raw conversation record before finishing. Do this even if the work felt "in progress"; future readers need the user-facing conversation context.
- Raw records should preserve user-visible turns and important assistant outcomes. Omit command transcripts, browser/tool chatter, and routine intermediate progress unless they affect interpretation.

Do not treat raw chats as final truth. Promote durable conclusions into `docs/knowledge/`.
